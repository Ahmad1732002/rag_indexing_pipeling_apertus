September 2022/ Soglio Scan
===========================



[https://hytac.arch.ethz.ch/elective/september-2022-soglio-scan/](https://hytac.arch.ethz.ch/elective/september-2022-soglio-scan/ "") by [https://hytac.arch.ethz.ch/author/fanny/](https://hytac.arch.ethz.ch/author/fanny/ "View all posts by ")

Foteini and William from the HYTAC Team went to Soglio, in the canton of Graubünden, to scan this small village.

This was done using a DJI drone and the Drone Display App to obtain a point\-cloud of the site. The data will be treated with Rhino and Blender during the HS22 elective course, in which students will create a digital model of the village, as well as print it with Prusa 3D printers.

This technology allow for quick and easy creation of a context model, which is very useful for the development of a future architectural project.
