HYTAC Farwell Exhibition
========================



[https://hytac.arch.ethz.ch/exhibitions/exhibition-farewell/](https://hytac.arch.ethz.ch/exhibitions/exhibition-farewell/ "") by [https://hytac.arch.ethz.ch/author/fanny/](https://hytac.arch.ethz.ch/author/fanny/ "View all posts by ")

 ![](https://hytac.arch.ethz.ch/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/250325_POSTER_farewell-exhibition_A2-724x1024.jpg)



HYTAC’s adventure at ETH comes to an end.

After five exciting years of exploring hybrid techniques in architecture design, the HYTAC research group invites you to its final exhibition: Workflow Cities.

From digital workflows to physical models, *Workflow Cities* presents a glimpse into the processes, experiments, and prototypes that have shaped HYTAC’s vision of contemporary urban design. This farewell moment is not just a look back, but a celebration of creative exploration, collaboration, and future\-thinking.

Where: HIL E3 Foyer, ETH Zürich

When: 28 April – 2 May 2025

Vernissage: Monday, 28 April at 6 PM

Come by, share a toast, and walk through the last chapter of HYTAC’s journey.
