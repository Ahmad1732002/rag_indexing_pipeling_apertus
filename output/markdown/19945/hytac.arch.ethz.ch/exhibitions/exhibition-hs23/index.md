HYTAC exhibition – HS23
=========================



[https://hytac.arch.ethz.ch/exhibitions/exhibition-hs23/](https://hytac.arch.ethz.ch/exhibitions/exhibition-hs23/ "") by [https://hytac.arch.ethz.ch/author/fanny/](https://hytac.arch.ethz.ch/author/fanny/ "View all posts by ")

After an intense seminarweek in October 2023, during which the first\-year architecture students took their first steps into the world of 3D printing, the HYTAC exhibition enabled them to showcase their work.  
No fewer than 350 boards show off the work accomplished during this intense week, but also during the first month of their studies: 3D models, CAD drawings, hybrid models, analog and/or digital sketches, renderings, … all the technologies presented during the HYTAC Basics course were highlighted !  
In collaboration with Chair Andrea Deplazes, the seminarweek, vernissage and exhibition were a real success!
